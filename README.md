# Nokia X GDG Concordia AI Coding Jam

**NOTE: This event is reserved for Nokia employees at the Kanata campus.**

Welcome! This is a fast-paced AI coding jam designed to boost your skills and spark your creativity.

## Schedule

- Check-in (12:00 p.m. - 12:10 p.m.)
- Presentation and Demo (12:10 p.m. - 12:20 p.m.)
- Build (12:20 p.m. - 1:10 p.m)
- **Submission Deadline (1:10 p.m.)**
- Voting Period (1:10 p.m. - 1:25 p.m)
- Winners announced (1:25 p.m. - 1:30 p.m.)

## Rules

- Maximum of 6 team members (all must RSVP)
- Every team member must possess a GitHub account
- Create a fork of the track repo of your choice (see [Steps](#steps))
- Submit your build by the deadline using the issue template

## Steps

1. Decide which track to follow (see [All Tracks](#all-tracks)):
    - Choose your own idea (track 09) OR
    - Choose a starter track (tracks 01 - 08)
2. Fork the track starter repo
3. (optional) Follow along the track's codelab
4. During the build:
    1. Setup your tools
    2. Plan your build
    3. Review your plan
    4. Build, build, build!
    5. Verify and fix
5. Submit your build by the deadline
